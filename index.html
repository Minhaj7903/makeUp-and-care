Financial Aid Available

The L Makeup Institute understands that financing an education can be one of the most complex and important issues that applicants face. For this reason, the L Makeup Institute’s Financial Aid Department can provide information and assistance to all applicants and families. Options for those who qualify include State Grants, Federal Grants, Federal Loans, VA benefits (Main Campus), scholarships, and loans through LMI.
